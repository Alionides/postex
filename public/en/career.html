As Yurtiçi Kargo, we believe that humans are the most important factor in business. Conscious work, honesty, patience, consistency, superior quality, development, unconditional love, support and encouragement are the basic principles of the culture created in Yurtiçi Kargo. As well the culture formed by us, Our Human Resources Policy is based on embracing our indispensable values. In the core of these values lie customer and quality-oriented service, participation, respect to others and social responsibility awareness. The prerequisites of success are common values of teams aiming for a specific target and the fact that all team members embrace these targets with their attitudes and decisions. Our strategy is to embrace the values that make us Yurtiçi Kargo and provide development; and also integrate new targets, attitudes and systems of the ever changing country and world conditions into corporate culture.

One of the most important tools of our participant, performance and development-oriented corporate culture is "Suggestion System" that came into effect in October, 2001. This application is a reflection our mission to increase personal responsibility in Yurtiçi Kargo Servisi A.Ş. general organization system and encourage sharing opinions on all levels. All employees can contribute to the Suggestion Development System as individuals without discrimination of title/function.

All Yurtiçi Kargo Employees

  • Endeavour to guarantee customer satisfaction and loyalty under all circumstances.
  • Are reliable and honest.
  • Are respectful to the society and environment.
  • Have quality awareness.
  • Are aware of their responsbilities.
  • Are result-oriented.
  • Are creative and participative.
  • Make suggestions and provide solutions.
  • Have efficient communication skills.
  • Bear these features in all aspects of life.

Our Human Resources Mission

Our mission is to determine processes that will make our employees happy and increase efficiency by evaluating the most valuable resource, humans, in the most efficient way possible; and contribute to reaching company targets with constant support to all other functions as strategic business partner.
