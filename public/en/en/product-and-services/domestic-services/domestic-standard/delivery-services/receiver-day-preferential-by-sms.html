  • RECEIVER DAY PREFERENTIAL BY SMS

After you hand in your mail to Yurtiçi Kargo, an SMS containing all necessary information such as delivery time, related Yurtiçi Kargo branch and invoice is sent to the recipient’s phone after the invoice is prepared. Thus, the recipient can make necessary adjustment related to the cargo with the provided information.

Both retail and corporate customers can take advantage of this special service and you can make necessary planning and adjustments in your business life in advance by obtaining information about your cargo.

Day selectable information service unit price is 2,07 TL. This service is available for all GSM operators.
