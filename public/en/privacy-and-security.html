COOKIE POLICY

As Yurtiçi Kargo Servisi A.Ş. (Hereinafter referred to as "YURTİÇİ KARGO".), we process the personal data and use cookies in order to provide a better user experience for our visitors by taking into consideration the right regarding their privacy and personal data through our website "www.yurticikargo.com.tr" ("Website"). This Cookie Usage Policy ("Policy") explains to our website visitors and users which types of cookies are used under which conditions.

This Policy is applied to any visitor regardless of which technologies, which methods and how you connect to the Website. Therefore, we kindly recommend you to read the Cookie Use Policy.

I. What does cookie mean?

Cookies are small data storage files stored on your computer, mobile phones, tablets or other mobile devices that you access the internet by websites.

II. What is a Cookie and For What Purposes Is It Used?

Data regarding your Website surf is stored in the cookie files. Thus, your accessed devices will remember this data the next time you use the Website. Therefore, cookies are necessary and crucial for you to use the Website effectively and easily.

Some Cookies are also used on the Website and the third-party websites to provide you with more appropriate services, products or offers.

The main purposes of using cookies;

  • The need of some data to use the Website more effectively,
  • To provide the functions of remembering your previous preferences, ensuring that you can easily access some content on the Website,
  • The need of your IP address and your personal data for the Company to fulfill its legal and contractual obligations, particularly those arising from the Law No. 5651 on the Regulation of Internet Broadcasts and the Fight Against Crimes Committed through These Broadcasts, and the Regulation on Principles and Procedures for the Broadcasts on the Internet.

III. What kind of data do we process with cookies?

Depending on their types, Cookies generally collect data about your browsing and user preferences on the device you access the Website. This data includes the pages you access, our services and products you browse, and all the data related to your surf on the Website.

IV. Which types of cookies do we use in what ways?

We use different types of cookies on the Website. These are the cookies and the analysis / performance cookies that are mandatory to use to ensure the operation of the Website.

TYPES OF COOKIES IN TERMS OF THEIR USE:

Mandatory Cookies: These cookies are essential for the proper functioning of the Website. These cookies are needed to manage the system and to prevent fraudulent transactions, and if they are blocked, the Website will not be able to operate on its own.

Function Cookies: These cookies are used to give you a more advanced and easier user experience. For example, their functions are to remember your previous preferences while providing you with easy access to some content on the Website. You can prevent the use of these cookies, which is detailed below.

Analysis / Performance Cookies: These cookies enable us to analyze the way you surf on the Website so that we can improve the Website by interacting with you. You can prevent the use of these cookies, which is detailed below.

Targeting / Advertising Cookies: These cookies are used to identify and present content that may be of interest to you, including advertising content. You can prevent the use of these cookies, which is detailed below. However, blocking these cookies may not completely block the advertising content and may provide general content ads instead of only the ads that may be of interest to you. These types of cookies are not used by YURTİÇİ KARGO yet you may be bearing cookies of the third parties before you visit the Website.

TYPES OF COOKIES IN TERMS OF THEIR STORAGE:

Persistent Cookies: These are the cookies that persist on a person's computer until a certain date or deleted by the user. These cookies are mostly used to measure users' site surfing and preferences.

Session Cookies: These cookies are used to allocate user visits to sessions and do not collect data from the user. The cookie is deleted when the user turns the webpage that he / she visited off or it has been passive for a certain period of time.

THE THIRD-PARTY COOKIES

Some third-party cookies are also used on our Website. Although these cookies are not located directly on the Website by YURTİÇİ KARGO, they are activated by users as a result of visiting other websites. Target and tracking cookies are used to help third parties provide services, primarily targeted advertising services, and to increase the effectiveness of such services. These cookies may remember the web pages and sites you visit and collect personal data, especially the IP address of the user. Only third-party cookies are used to collect data to remember your interests and demographic data, and serve you targeted ads, improve ads, determine the number of visits and ad impressions, other uses of ad services, the ratio between these ad impressions and interactions with ad services by the Website.

The Website is directly connected to the server of the selected social network when you provide connection to some social networks, visit the Website from social plugins and use these plugins. Then, the content offered by the plugin is transmitted directly from social networks to your web browser and added to the website you are visiting. Thus, the relevant social network can access and process your data and combine it with the data of your account in the relevant social network.

Please note that we have no influence or control over the scope of data processed by social networks through plugins. For more information on what purpose, what methods and for how long social networks will process your personal data, please carefully review the policies for processing personal data published by the respective social networks.

V. How can you prevent the use of cookies?

While some cookies are mandatory for the website to work, you can prevent the use of cookies other than these. However, please note that even in this case, the Website may not function properly and you may not be able to use all its features.

You need to change your internet browser settings to prevent the use of cookies. These changes vary according to the device and internet browser you use. Information on what steps to take to prevent the use of cookies via different internet browsers are listed below:

  • Internet Explorer
    1. Turn the desktop on and tap or click on the Internet Explorer icon on the taskbar.
    2. Tap or click on the Tools button and Internet options.
    3. Tap or click on the Privacy tab, then move the slider up under Settings to block all cookies and tap or click on the OK button.
  • Microsoft Edge
    1. Click on the section with three dots in the upper right corner of your Microsoft Edge browser and go to the Settings section.
    2. After selecting the Items to Clean from the currently displayed window that comes up, select the sections you want to clean from the window that comes up next.
    3. There are many sections here. You can start the cleaning process by choosing the one you want.
  • Google Chrome
    1. Open Chrome on your computer.
    2. Click on Other Settings at the top right.
    3. Click on Advanced at the bottom.
    4. Under "Privacy and security," click on Content settings.
    5. Click on Cookies.
    6. Search for the name of the Website under "All cookies and website data".
    7. Click on the Remove icon on the right of the website.
  • Mozilla Firefox
    1. Click on the Firefox Menu button and select Options.
    2. Select the Privacy and Security panel and proceed to the History section.
    3. Change the Firefox setting to "Use custom settings for the past".
    4. Click on the "Show cookies" button. The cookies window will be displayed.
    5. Type the name of the website whose cookies you want to delete into the Search field. Cookies matching your search will be displayed.
    6. Select the cookie (s) you want to delete and click on "Delete selected items" button.
    7. Click on the close button to close the Cookies window. Then close the about: preferences page.
  • Safari
    1. Select Safari> Preferences.
    2. Click on Privacy.
    3. Click on Website Data.
    4. Select one or more websites, and then click on Delete or Delete All.
    5. You can find more information about how to perform this procedure in other main web browsers at “https://www.esb.org.tr/cerez-bellek” or “https://www.aboutcookies.org”.
